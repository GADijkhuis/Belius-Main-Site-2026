# Belius Main Site 2026 ⛵

Belius Sailing Instruction focuses on promoting sailing, especially among youth. Our approach is centered around collaboration: together with the sailors, we continuously explore what works best for both the individual and the team. This includes training methods and guidance during practice sessions.

Enjoyment is always our top priority. From that foundation, we support sailors in developing their skills and confidence on the water.

In addition to training sessions, we provide guidance during competitions and events. At the start of the season, we discuss the competition calendar with sailors and their parents, deciding together which events to attend as part of the Belius sailing team. Participation in other events is always optional and independent.

Sailors are also welcome to join only the training sessions if they prefer—flexibility is key. As long as we enjoy sailing, we believe growth will naturally follow.
